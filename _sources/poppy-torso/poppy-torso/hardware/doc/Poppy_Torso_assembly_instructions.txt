#### 1.4.1 Requirement

![](img/poppy_torso_assembly_BOM.jpg)

**Sub-assemblies:**
- Trunk
- Left arm
- Right arm

**3D printed parts:**
- Left shoulder
- right shoulder

**Cables:**
- 2x 3P 200mm

**Robotis parts:**
- 48x Bolts M2x3

**Motor configuration:**
- 1x Alimentation 12V
- 1x SMPS2Dynamixel
- 1x USB2Dynamixel or USB2AX
- A computer...
